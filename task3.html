Rohit Sharma

Indian Cricketer

About Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma is an Indian cricketer who has represented the national team in all formats of the game. He is known for his elegant batting style and has achieved numerous records in international cricket.

Born: April 30, 1987

Place of Birth: Nagpur, Maharashtra, India

Role: Batsman

Teams: Mumbai Indians, India

Achievements

  • First player to score three double centuries in One Day Internationals (ODIs).
  • Joint-fastest century in T20 Internationals (35 balls).
  • Won several Indian Premier League (IPL) titles with Mumbai Indians.
  • Named ICC ODI Cricketer of the Year in 2019.
  • Currently holds the record for the highest individual score in ODIs (264 runs).

Tribute to Rohit Sharma

Rohit Sharma's contribution to Indian cricket has been immense. His ability to score big runs consistently and his leadership qualities make him one of the most respected players in the world.

He continues to inspire young cricketers and remains a key player for both the Indian national team and the Mumbai Indians in the IPL.

Let's celebrate Rohit Sharma's achievements and wish him continued success in his cricketing career!
